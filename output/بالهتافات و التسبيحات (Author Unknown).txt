على إبليس النصر أكيد
Intal malikil waheed i'lan mulkak leena yizeed wa'ala hisab dam issaleeb ala iblis innasri akeed

Nirfa' ismak yabnal lah dab sultanak leena haya tuhnal ismak kuly jibah tasbeehak min kuly shifah
